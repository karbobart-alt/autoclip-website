Information we collect

To turn your footage into clips, AutoClip needs to collect a bit more than a typical website. We collect:

  • Account information — your name, email address, and password (or login provider) when you create an account.
  • Uploaded content — the video files you upload, along with the clips, captions, titles, and hashtags AutoClip generates from them.
  • Connected accounts — when TikTok auto-publish becomes available, the access tokens needed to post on your behalf, granted only after you explicitly connect your account.
  • Usage data — basic product analytics, such as which features you use, to help us improve AutoClip.

How AI processing works

When you upload a video, it's processed on our secure servers so our AI models can analyze the audio and visuals, identify clip-worthy moments, and generate captions, titles, and hashtags. This processing is automated; a person does not manually review your footage unless you contact support about a specific file.

We do not use your uploaded video content to train models shared across other customers without your consent, and you can request that a file be excluded from any model-improvement process at any time.

How we use information

We use the information above to:

  • Generate your clips, captions, titles, and hashtags.
  • Operate, maintain, and improve AutoClip's AI selection and captioning quality.
  • Once available, publish or schedule clips to TikTok at your direction.
  • Provide customer support and respond to your requests.
  • Send product updates, which you can opt out of at any time.

Third-party services

We work with a small number of infrastructure providers — cloud hosting and storage, AI model providers, a payment processor, and (once live) the TikTok API for publishing. Each receives only the data required to perform its function, and none are permitted to use your content for their own purposes.

We do not sell your personal information or your video content to anyone.

Data retention

Uploaded source videos are retained for as long as needed to generate your clips, plus a short buffer for re-processing, and are then automatically deleted unless you choose to keep them in your library. Generated clips remain in your account until you delete them or close your account. You can delete any file manually at any time.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete the personal data and content we hold about you, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise any of these rights, reach out through our contact page.

Children's privacy

AutoClip is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us and we'll remove it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as AutoClip evolves — particularly as features like TikTok auto-publish move from development to launch. We'll notify registered users of material changes by email and update the date at the top of this page.

Contact us

Questions about this policy can be sent through our contact page or to privacy@autoclip.app.
